Deep Dive into SGLang

Deep Dive into SGLang

A two-volume systems book on SGLang's inference runtime, from online request scheduling, KV memory, prefix reuse, and decoding algorithms to quantization, distributed serving, kernels, hardware backends, benchmarking, correctness, and extension work.

Buy Volume I Buy Volume II Companion Code

Volume I

Runtime, Scheduling, Memory, and Decoding

Volume I explains SGLang as a live inference system: requests arrive continuously, GPU memory is limited, and each decode step reshapes the next scheduling choice.

Paperback Hardcover Kindle

Volume II

Quantization, Distributed Serving, Kernels, and Measurement

Volume II follows SGLang beyond the core runtime path into scale, specialization, and measurement, including adapters, expert routing, disaggregation, hardware backends, multimodal workloads, and tests.
